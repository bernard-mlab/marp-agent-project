## Distinguishing Deception Modes

![bg right:40%](../assets/marpx/thinkers/frankfurt-harry.jpg)

**Liar**
- Knows the truth
- Intends contradiction

**Bullshitter**
- Indifferent to truth value
- Optimizes persuasion effect

> **Callout:** Indifference to truth can be more corrosive than direct lying.
